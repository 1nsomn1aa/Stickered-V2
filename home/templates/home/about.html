About Stickered

Where creativity and car culture collide

Our Story

Stickered was born from a garage filled with drift cars, race weekends, and late-night sketch sessions. What began as a passion project turned into a home for creativity, where self-expression meets quality.

Built by Enthusiasts

We're more than a brand — we're part of the scene. From limited edition windshield banners to clean daily-driver graphics, Stickered is built for the ones who live and breathe cars.

Our Mission

We empower individuals to stand out, to create, and to roll with style. We offer high-end, durable products that go from city streets to race tracks — and never back down.
